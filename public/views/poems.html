He Waltz Into Her Heart

Hands folded in her lap. Chrystal sat in a row of empty chairs at a
dance studio. Why did I come without Liz? It feels awkward to be
here without her. She fidgeted. Oh yes, I promised to go with her to
her sorority senior ball. Even though I don’t enjoy dancing. Being
her older sister comes with sacrifices. Suddenly a young man towered
over her.

"Join me, please." He extended his hand...
